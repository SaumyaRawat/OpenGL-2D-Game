			Central Park!

Bring out the prankster in you! Break the birds' eggs floating on the screen and get points based on
how far the eggs are.

Red and his cousins will try to block you though :P

a : Move the canon up
b: Move the canon down
Space Bar : Launch the shell from cannon
f: Launch the shell faster
s: Launch the shell at a slower speed.

Right Click and drag OR Left and Right arrow keys to pan 
Scroll Up and Down OR Up and Down arrow keys to zoom
